About the Legend

Lata Mangeshkar, also known as the 'Nightingale of India', was one of the most
versatile singers in the Indian film industry. Lata was born on September 28, 1929,
to classical singer and theatre artist Pandit Deenanath Mangeshkar and Shevanti in
Indore,Madhya Pradesh.
